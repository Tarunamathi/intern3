## Usage

### For Trainers
1. Navigate to `/trainer/certificates`
2. Configure branding (company name, logo, institute name)
3. Choose one of the 10 attractive templates
4. Select batch and trainee
5. Fill in completion details and grade
6. View live preview
7. Issue certificate
8. Download or view issued certificates

### Template Selection Strategy
- Use **Classic Elegance** or **Premium Gold** for traditional organizations
- Use **Modern Professional** or **Corporate Blue** for business training
- Use **Agricultural Green** for farming/agriculture programs
- Use **Ocean Blue** for environmental/marine programs
- Use **Vibrant Coral** for creative/modern programs
- Use **Royal Purple** for academic/formal programs
- Use **Emerald Excellence** for premium/executive programs
